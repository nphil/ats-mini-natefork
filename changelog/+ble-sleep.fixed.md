Fix crash when the device enters light sleep while a BLE client is connected. The teardown sequence in NordicUART::stop() now sets `started = false` before tearing down GATT state, gracefully disconnects active clients with a short propagation delay, and guards onDisconnect against post-stop invocation (which was double-releasing a binary_semaphore and dereferencing a destroyed BLEServer). Ble.cpp helpers also bail out cleanly when the controller is mid-teardown.
